English Phrase = French Phrase


1] Good Morning ! = Bonjour !

2] Good Evening ! = Bonsoir !

3] Hi ! = Salut

4] A tout a l'heure  = See You Later 

5] A bientot  = See You Soon 

6] Au revoir = Goodbye

7] Comment tu t'appelles ? = What is your name ?

8] Je m'appelle Vinit = My name is Vinit
